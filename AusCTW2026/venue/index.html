Venue

AusCTW2026 will be hosted in the Rātā Building, on the campus of the University of Canterbury, Ilam — Ōtautahi | Christchurch, New Zealand.

New Zealand is awesome! Spend some time seeing the place while you are here. Links to tourist information will appear here.

Accommodation

We have negotiated a convenient and affordable accommodation option within an easy stroll across the green Ilam campus of the University of Canterbury to our conference hub. Breakfast is included.

Please see the booking details on the Humanitix website when you are registering for the conference.
